<h1 align="center">PRESIDENT UNIVERSITY</h1>

<p align="center" >Welcome to the most international university in Indonesia!</p>

President University (formerly known as Sekolah Tinggi Teknik Cikarang) is a private university located in Jababeka Industrial Estate in [Cikarang](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cikarang), Indonesia. The university officially began its operations on April 16, 2004, through Ministerial Decree 54/D/0/2004.

  <h2 align="center" style="margin-top: 0px;">Why We Decided to Build This Website</h2>
</p>

With a vast variety of students and faculty, we wanted to create a website that provides a comprehensive overview of the university's programs and activities, especially on the profile of its students and faculty. We know that a website like this would be invaluable for prospective students and faculty alike, and we are excited to share it with the community. That's why, we, the students of Information Technology at President University, built this website functioned as profiles for all students and faculty.
